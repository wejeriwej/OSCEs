This is a website for medical students to practice taking histories on a simulated patient. (Warning: The voice recording feature only works on Google Chrome.)

Case 1:

You are a Junior Doctor in a GP, and your next patient is a 58 year old male that is complaining of chest pain. Please take a full history.

Press the Microphone button and start speaking to record a question. Then press Submit Question to get a response


Once the consultation has ended:


  1. What examination would you perform on this patient?

    Click to watch a video of how to do the examination



  2. Please give us a brief summary of the history and the examination (1-2 minutes)

    Press the Microphone button and start speaking to record your summary. Then press Submit Summary to get a response



  3. What are your top 3 differentials?



  4. What blood tests would you do on this patient, and why?

    Press the Microphone button and start speaking to record your summary. Then press Submit Question to get a response



  5. What other investigations would you do?

    Press the Microphone button and start speaking to record your answer. Then press Submit Question to get a response



  6. What risk factors are there for developing a heart attack? (name 5)

    Press the Microphone button and start speaking to record your answer. Then press Submit Question to get a response



  7. What treatment options are there for those with Myocardial Infarctions?

    Press the Microphone button and start speaking to record your answer. Then press Submit Question to get a response
